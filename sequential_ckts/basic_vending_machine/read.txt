->design specification:
a simple digital circuit is to be designed for the
coin acceptor of an electronic newspaper vending machine:
-assume that the newspaper costs 15Rs.
-the coin acceptor takes only 5Rs and 10Rs.
-exact change must be provided. The acceptor does not
return extra money.
-valid combinations including order of coins are 
(5, 10), (5, 5, 5) (10, 5). 2x10 Rs are valid but 
acceptor does not return money.
